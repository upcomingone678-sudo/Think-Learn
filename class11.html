Learning to Measure is Fun!

We'll learn how to find out how long, how heavy, and how much things hold, using our hands, feet, and eyes!

Our Fun Topics

How Long? (Length)

Compare tall vs. short objects. Measure your desk using your **handspans** or your room using **foot-steps**! No scales needed.

How Heavy? (Weight)

We learn to check if an apple is heavy or a feather is light. Compare two objects in your hands and guess which one is **heavier**.

How Long Ago? (Time)

Understand the difference between **Morning, Afternoon, and Night**. Learn to look at the short hand on the clock to tell the hour.

How Much? (Capacity)

Using water and sand, we will see which container holds **more** and which holds **less**. Learning capacity without needing litres!
